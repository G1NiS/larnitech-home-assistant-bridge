# Larnitech HA Bridge

Local Home Assistant integration for Larnitech-compatible smart home systems.

## Community Edition

The HACS version is the free community baseline:

- local API2 WebSocket connection;
- native Home Assistant entities;
- lights and dimmers;
- common sensors and binary sensors;
- valves and switches;
- fancoils as simple ON/OFF fan entities.

## Notes

This integration runs locally and does not require a license key.
